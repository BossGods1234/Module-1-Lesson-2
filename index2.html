About Giannis

Early life

As the son of immigrants from Nigeria,[13][14][15] Giannis Sina Ugo Adetokunbo[16][17] was born in Athens, Greece, on December 6, 1994.[18] His father was a soccer player and his mother was a high jumper in Nigeria. Three years earlier, they had moved from Lagos, leaving their firstborn son, Francis, under the care of his grandparents.[19] Adetokunbo grew up in the Athens neighborhood of Sepolia[15] in a primarily Nigerian household.[20] His parents, as immigrants without work permits,[21] could not easily find work, so Giannis and his older brother Thanasis helped by hawking watches, handbags, and sunglasses in the streets.[22] In 2007, Adetokunbo started playing basketball.[23]
